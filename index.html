About BAUST CSE Hub

BAUST CSE Resource Hub is a centralized platform built for CSE students of Bangladesh Army University of Science and Technology (BAUST) Khulna.

Finding quality free resources across all subjects can be overwhelming. This hub organizes everything — YouTube playlists, notes, websites, practice platforms, and GitHub projects — for every course in the CSE curriculum.

All resources are 100% free. Built with ❤️ for BAUST CSE students by a Pioneer Batch student.
